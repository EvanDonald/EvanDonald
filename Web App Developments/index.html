About:

This totally unique approach to urban farming allows you to skip the hassle of raising baby chicks, instead proceeding to fully developed,
egg producing hens delivered right to your door. For a small fee, Rent-a-Chicken will set you up with everything you need for your backyard chicken coop.
As an added bonus, Rent-a-Chicken will even tag and house your chicken over the winter, ensuring a joyful spring reunion.
